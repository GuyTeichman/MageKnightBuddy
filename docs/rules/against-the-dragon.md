# Against the Dragon

Source: `Mage-Knight-The-Apocalypse-Dragon---Rulebook-WEB.pdf`, pages 31–34.

A scenario where the Apocalypse Dragon actively destroys sites and attacks players each Round while you race to reach and defeat it in its lair. Not a v1 target (v1 is Solo Conquest only) — captured here as reference for when this scenario gets implemented.

## Overview

- Length: Four rounds (2 days and 2 nights).
- Purpose: reach the Apocalypse Dragon's lair as it attacks from the air, then defeat it on the ground to protect its sanctuary.

Key facts relevant to scoring/setup only:
- The Dragon always takes the last turn each Round; it stops taking turns once first attacked in its lair, or once "End of the Round" is announced.
- Core non-city tile **3** is always included in setup — revealing it places the Dragon (covering the central wasteland space and the 2 spaces above it) and marks its lair.
- Optional Blitz variant: start with 1 Fame and 2(+1) Reputation, +1 Fame per level gained, one extra Source die and one extra Unit in the offer.

## Setup

### Competitive

- Players: 2 to 4.
- Map Shape: Wedge, Wedge, or Open limited to 4 columns.
- Countryside tiles: 6, 7, or 9. Core city tiles: 1. Core non-city tiles: 1, 2, or 3 (always include tile 3).
- City: friendly — each player puts one Shield token on it, no-one is its leader.
- Each Dragon head's level = number of players + 1.

### Cooperative

- Players: 2 to 4.
- Map Shape: Fully open.
- Countryside tiles: 6, 8, or 10. Core city tiles: 1. Core non-city tiles: 2, 3, or 4 (always include tile 3).
- City: friendly, same as Competitive.
- Each Dragon head's level = number of players + 3.
- Dummy Player: one standard Dummy Player. Cards/Skills: remove the four competitive Spells and the one competitive interactive Skill from each player's Skill tokens; use cooperative interactive Skill tokens if available.
- Tactics: Dummy Player takes a random Tactic first, then the players choose theirs; at the end of the first Day *and* Night, agree and remove one player-used Tactic from the game. Team Rules apply, everyone is one team.

### Solo

- Players: 1.
- Map Shape: Wedge.
- Countryside tiles: 5. Core city tiles: 1. Core non-city tiles: 2 (always include tile 3).
- City: friendly — both the player and the Dummy Player put a Shield token on it, no-one is its leader.
- Each Dragon head is level 4.
- Dummy Player: one standard Dummy Player. Cards/Skills: same removal/substitution as Cooperative.
- Tactics: player always chooses first; Dummy Player takes one random remaining card. At the end of each Day/Night, remove both used Tactic cards from the game.

## Scenario end

When the Apocalypse Dragon is defeated: all players (Competitive, including whoever defeated it) or all players except the Dummy Player (Cooperative) get one last turn; Solo: the player gets one last turn (the Dummy Player does not). The Round ends immediately if the Round limit is hit mid-sequence.

## Scoring

### Competitive

Success = Dragon defeated. Standard Achievements Scoring, plus for each head except the Control head:
- Each player scores Fame equal to their count of Shield tokens on that head's large enemy token.
- Highest count → +5 Fame Greatest Slayer of that head (ties broken toward whoever's Shield token sits on the highest level).

### Cooperative

One team score = lowest Fame among all players as base, then standard Achievements Scoring except only the highest-scoring player counts per category (no Titles). Plus:
- +5 points per head defeated (excluding the Control head).
- +15 points more if all heads were defeated.
- +30 points per Round finished before the Round limit.
- +1 point per unflipped card in the Dummy Player's Deed deck.
- +5 points if "End of the Round" wasn't announced yet in your last Round.

### Solo

Base = your Fame. Standard Achievements Scoring, no Titles. Plus:
- +5 points per head you defeated (excluding the Control head).
- +15 points more if you defeated all heads.
- +30 points per Round finished before the Round limit.
- +1 point per unflipped card in the Dummy Player's Deed deck.
- +5 points if "End of the Round" wasn't announced yet in your last Round.

## Outcome (Won/Lost)

Won iff the Apocalypse Dragon was defeated; Lost otherwise. A score is always computed either way.
